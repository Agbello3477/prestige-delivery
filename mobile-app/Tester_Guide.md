# Prestige Delivery - Mobile Tester Guide 📱

Thank you for helping us test the Prestige Delivery mobile application. Please follow the steps below to set up and test the app.

## 1. Prerequisites
- Download the **Expo Go** app from the [App Store (iOS)](https://apps.apple.com/app/expo-go/id982107779) or [Google Play Store (Android)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=host.exp.exponent).

## 2. Installation & Access
- Open the Expo Go app.
- (A link or QR code will be provided by the administrator).
- Join the project as a Guest or using your provided test account.

## 3. Account Roles to Test
The app supports three different user experiences:
- **Customer**: Order items from vendors (Food, Pharmacy, E-commerce) or request a delivery.
- **Rider**: Receive delivery requests, navigate to pickup/dropoff points, and confirm deliveries.
- **Partner (Vendor)**: Manage incoming orders.

## 4. Key Test Scenarios

### A. Customer Flow
1. **Register/Login** as a Customer.
2. Browse the **Vendor List**.
3. Select a vendor and add items to your **Cart**.
4. **Place an Order** with your delivery address.
5. Go to **Active Deliveries** to track your order.

### B. Rider Flow
1. **Register/Login** as a Rider.
2. Toggle your status to **Online**.
3. Wait for a **Delivery Request** and tap **Accept**.
4. Follow the map to the **Pickup** point.
5. Tap **Pickup** and then move to the **Dropoff** point.
6. Tap **Confirm** and provide a **Signature** or **Video Proof** to complete the delivery.

### C. Security Features
1. Go to **Settings/Profile**.
2. Test the **Change Password** flow.
3. Test **Logout** and **Login**.

## 5. Reporting Issues
If you encounter any bugs, crashes, or UI issues:
- Take a screenshot or screen recording.
- Note down your device model and OS version.
- Send the report to the dev team.
